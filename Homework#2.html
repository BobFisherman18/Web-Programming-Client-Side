CS3, the Intro computer survey class at Santa Monica College, is offered every semester. Several sections are avalible. Nevertheless, the popularity of the class has meant in the past that not all students wanting to take the class could be admitted.

The primary textbook for this course is Computers, Tools for and Infomation Age by H.L.Capron
